# Sales-Analysis-With-Tableau-

This project is an interactive Sales Analysis Dashboard created using Tableau Public.
The dashboard provides a clear overview of sales performance across states, months, regions, age groups, discounts, and gender-based categories.
It helps stakeholders quickly understand trends, patterns, and key insights for better decision-making.

Kpi Indicators:
nalyze revenue trends over time
Understand regional sales distribution
Identify high-performing states
Study customer behavior based on age and gender
Analyze the impact of discounts on quantity ordered


Revenue per State (Map)
Visualizes total revenue across different states
Helps identify top and low-performing states

Revenue per Month (Line Chart)
Shows monthly revenue trends
Helps detect seasonal patterns and growth trends.


Revenue by Region (Pie Chart)
Displays revenue contribution from:
West
Midwest
South
Northeast

Age-wise Revenue Distribution (Bar Chart)
Shows revenue contribution by different age groups
Helps understand which age group generates more sales

Discount Analysis (Histogram)
Displays quantity ordered across different discount percentage ranges
Helps analyze how discounts affect sales volume

Gender & Category Analysis (Bar Chart)
Shows total sales by product category for each gender
Useful for customer segmentation and targeted marketing

Certain regions contribute significantly higher revenue

Middle-age customer groups generate higher revenue

Product category preferences vary by gender
